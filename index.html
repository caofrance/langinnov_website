    LangInnov is the main provider of innovative, multi-platform, and comprehensive educational applications.
    Its primary focus is on emergent bilinguals and bilingual communities in the US.

    • Discover our BLAST application

    The BLAST application

    Bilingual Literacy Assessment and Skills Tracker

    Language and literacy assessment for bilinguals

    It measures reading, writing, listening and oral skills. The assessment is intuitive, adapting to each student on the go, and is graded automatically.

    A user-friendly and fully automated application

    It includes a platform where teachers and parents collaborate and where each student receives a personalized bundle of analytics and activities.

    Capacity of assessing oral skills in children

    This is accomplished by using a speech recognizer that will be refined by integrating machine learning algorithms trained on children speech data.

    About US

    Dr. Raña and Dr. Cao met in New York City while pursuing doctoral studies in linguistics and after a few years of work in research, teaching, and business, they realized that there was a societal need for an app such as BLAST.
